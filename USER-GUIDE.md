## Introduction

The Financial Education Portal is designed to provide comprehensive, unbiased information about financial services, banking platforms, fintech solutions, and gaming platforms. The platform prioritizes educational content and transparency while maintaining strict compliance with regulatory requirements.

### What You Can Do

As a visitor, you can:

- Browse educational articles about financial platforms
- Learn about different platform categories
- Understand regulatory frameworks and consumer protection
- Read risk warnings and compliance information
- Access payment method guides
- View testimonials and reviews
- Manage your privacy preferences
- Subscribe to updates and promotions

## Frequently Asked Questions

### General Questions

**Q: Do I need to create an account?**
A: No, browsing educational content doesn't require an account.

**Q: Is the site free to use?**
A: Yes, all educational content is completely free.

**Q: How often is content updated?**
A: Content is regularly reviewed and updated. Check article dates for freshness.

**Q: Can I trust the information?**
A: All content follows strict editorial standards. See `/editorial-standards` for details.

### Privacy Questions

**Q: What data do you collect?**
A: Only essential usage data and cookies based on your consent. See `/privacy` for details.

**Q: Do you sell my data?**
A: No, we never sell user data to third parties.

**Q: Can I delete my data?**
A: Yes, contact us to exercise your privacy rights.

### Content Questions

**Q: Who writes the content?**
A: Content is created by financial education specialists and subject matter experts.

**Q: Can I suggest topics?**
A: Yes, we welcome topic suggestions via our contact form.

**Q: Do you receive commissions?**
A: Some promotional content may include affiliate relationships, which are always disclosed.

### Technical Questions

**Q: Which browsers are supported?**
A: All modern browsers (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge) from the last 2 years.

**Q: Why isn't a page loading?**
A: Try refreshing the page or clearing your browser cache. Contact support if issues persist.

**Q: Is my connection secure?**
A: Yes, the site uses HTTPS encryption for all connections.

**Q: Can I use the site offline?**
A: Limited offline functionality is available through browser caching.

## Glossary

**SRIJ** - Serviço de Regulação e Inspeção de Jogos (Portuguese Gaming Regulation Service)

**RLS** - Row-Level Security (database security feature)

**Lead Gen Page** - Lead Generation Page, specialized landing page

**Cookie** - Small text file stored by your browser

**GDPR** - General Data Protection Regulation (EU privacy law)

**Analytics** - Data about how visitors use the site

**Iframe** - Embedded content from external sources
